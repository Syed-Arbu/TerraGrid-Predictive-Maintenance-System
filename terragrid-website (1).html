Risk Factor Radar
Model ExplainabilityWeighted Multi-Factor Scoring

The TerraGrid ML model uses a weighted risk scoring algorithm based on real-time sensor analysis, equipment age, and degradation trend detection. Each factor contributes a percentage to the final 0–100 risk score.

🌡️
Temperature Factor (35% weight)
Sustained temperatures above the 55°C baseline are normalised against the 100°C danger threshold. Values exceeding 85°C flag potential bearing or insulation failure.
📳
Vibration Factor (40% weight)
The highest-weighted factor. Excessive vibration indicates bearing wear, mechanical imbalance, or structural fatigue. The model also detects upward trends to catch accelerating degradation early.
⚡
Load Factor (25% weight)
Prolonged operation above 85% capacity generates thermal stress and accelerates component wear. Sustained overloading adds directly to the risk score.
📅
Age Penalty (+0–20 pts)
Equipment degrades with age. The model adds 2.5 risk points per year of service, capped at 20 points, reflecting manufacturer lifecycle data.
